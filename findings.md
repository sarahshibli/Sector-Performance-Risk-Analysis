# Findings

## Performance
Financials significantly outperformed other sectors, growing from $1 to over $3.

Technology also showed strong growth with more consistent performance.

## Risk
Healthcare had the highest volatility, indicating frequent fluctuations.

Consumer Staples had the lowest volatility, making it the most stable sector.

## Risk-Return Tradeoff
Financials represent a high-risk, high-return sector, while Technology provides a more balanced profile. Consumer Staples offer stability but lower growth potential.
