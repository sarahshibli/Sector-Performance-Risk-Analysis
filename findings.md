# Findings

## Performance
Financials significantly outperformed other sectors, growing from $1 to over $3 over the analysis period.

Technology also delivered strong performance, showing steady and more consistent growth compared to other sectors.

## Risk
Healthcare exhibited the highest volatility, indicating frequent fluctuations in returns and a higher level of risk.

Consumer Staples had the lowest volatility, making it the most stable and least risky sector.

## Risk-Return Tradeoff
Financials represent a high-risk, high-return sector, while Technology offers a more balanced risk-return profile. Consumer Staples provide stability but at the cost of lower overall returns.

## Investment Insight
Based on the analysis, Technology serves as a strong core investment due to its consistent growth and moderate risk. Financials, while more volatile, offer higher return potential and can enhance overall portfolio performance.

A combination of both sectors provides balanced exposure to growth and upside potential, aligning with a growth-oriented investment strategy.
